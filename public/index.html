PO Sync is Here: Effortless Ordering & Imports

Tired of manually writing purchase orders and importing products to your POS? PO Sync automates ordering and product creation, so you can focus on driving your business.

Import in one click
  1. Go to Stock Control > Import, and you’ll now find your NuORDER POs ready to import.
  2. With a single click, get brand-approved product images, descriptions, and UPCs synced into your inventory.
Order with Ease
  1. Go to Stock Control > Order Stock
  2. Select a “Network Supplier”
  3. Confirm your delivery outlet
  4. Add products to your order
  5. Click Save and send to NuORDER for processing
  6. Once processed, review orders in NuORDER or your POS
That’s it! A seamless way to order directly from suppliers in your network. Learn more about creating orders and importing orders in our Help Center.
